About This Course

This interactive AI Literacy Course for Primary School Teachers was designed to provide educators with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to understand AI concepts and integrate them appropriately into primary education.

The course is structured to respect teachers' busy schedules with time-efficient modules, progress tracking, varied assessment types, and practical classroom applications.

Course Features

  • Interactive navigation between slides
  • Knowledge check quizzes with immediate feedback
  • Visual aids and diagrams
  • Practical classroom activities
  • Blue and white color scheme for clear readability
